# Platform Rules — Thailand Moto Rent

All rental partners must accept these rules before listing on the platform.

## 1. License & identity

- Valid motorcycle driving license required at pickup
- Passport or Thai ID copy stored per booking
- International licenses: accepted only from approved countries (list TBD)

## 2. Insurance

- Minimum third-party liability insurance mandatory
- Partner must show proof of coverage during onboarding
- Optional full coverage can be offered as add-on

## 3. Pricing transparency

- Daily rate, deposit, and known extras shown before payment
- No hidden fees at pickup
- Currency: THB (฿) displayed prominently

## 4. Vehicle standards

- Safety checklist before each rental (brakes, lights, tires, helmet)
- Bikes above agreed age limit require extra inspection
- Maintenance bikes cannot be listed as available

## 5. Cancellation & refunds

- Free cancellation up to 24h before pickup (default)
- Partner-specific stricter policies must be disclosed upfront
- No-show policy: deposit handling defined in partner agreement

## 6. Commission & payments

- Platform commission deducted per completed booking
- Payout schedule: weekly (TBD)
- Chargebacks handled per platform dispute process

## 7. Partner conduct

- Accurate listings (model, cc, photos)
- Response to booking requests within 2 hours (business hours)
- Repeated violations → suspension from platform
